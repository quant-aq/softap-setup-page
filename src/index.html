WiFi Setup

Let's connect your QuantAQ device to the Wifi.

  1. Plug-in your device.
  2. Make sure the LED on the microcontroller is blinking blue. If it isn't, press and hold the 'S' button until it does.
  3. Disconnect from your WiFi and Connect to the new WiFi hotspot it creates. It will appear as Photon-XXXX on your network list.
  4. Press Next.
